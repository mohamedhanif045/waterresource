About This Project

The Water Resource Management in Northern Kenya project aims to address the critical challenges of water scarcity and quality in the arid and semi-arid regions of Northern Kenya. Through comprehensive monitoring and management practices, we strive to ensure sustainable water resources for communities, agriculture, and the environment.

Our mission is to provide reliable data on water sources, usage, and quality, and to promote effective conservation practices. By leveraging technology and community engagement, we aim to make a positive impact on the water security and resilience of the region.

Our key objectives include:

  • Monitoring water sources and usage to provide accurate and up-to-date information.
  • Improving water quality through testing and treatment initiatives.
  • Promoting water conservation practices among communities and stakeholders.
  • Collaborating with government and non-governmental organizations to develop sustainable water management solutions.

Our Team

We are a dedicated team of water resource experts, environmental scientists, and community development professionals committed to improving water management in Northern Kenya. Our diverse backgrounds and expertise enable us to tackle the complex challenges of water scarcity and quality in the region.

Our team includes:

  • Hydrologists and water resource engineers who specialize in water source management and conservation.
  • Environmental scientists who focus on water quality testing and treatment.
  • Community development specialists who work closely with local communities to implement sustainable water practices.
  • Data analysts and technologists who develop and maintain our monitoring systems and provide actionable insights.
